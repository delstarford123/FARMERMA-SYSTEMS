The Challenge

Africa’s Agricultural Transformation Challenge

Africa holds 60% of the world’s uncultivated arable land, yet the continent imports billions of dollars in food every year.

Structural challenges across markets, finance, infrastructure, and climate resilience continue to constrain the sector. Farmerman Systems works at the intersection of data, policy, and investment to unlock Africa’s agricultural potential.
